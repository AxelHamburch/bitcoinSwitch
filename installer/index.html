Installation and configuring

Use this URL only in "Microsoft EDGE" or "Google Crome" Browser!

Software Konfiguration in two chapters.

Chapter one: uploade the Software to run the pleptap Device

Chapter two: uploade the software to run the plebtap with your Wifi

Chapter one

Step 1

Connect USB AND! use the push-button at the LOWER-SIDE underneath the plebtap for 3 seconds.

Step 2

klick on "Flash" and connect your Device as shown in the window. (CP2102 USB to UART Bridge)

Step 3

install

After install disconnect the device

Chapter two

Step 1

Connect USB AND! use the push-button at the TOP-SIDE for 3 seconds.

Step 2

Klick on "Config" and "Select Template" -> Bitcoin-Switch

Step 3

Fill in "WiFi SSID" your WLAN ID like "plebtap"

Step 4

Fill in "WiFi Password" your WLAN Password like "XXXXXXX"

Step 5

Fill in "Copy link from LNURLDevices extension in LNbits" your device link like "ws://legend.lnbits.com/api/v1/ws/xxxxxxxxxxxxx"

Notice: Line "LNURL" "true" does not change

Step 6

klick on "Save to device"

Do you see the progress bar? perfect :-) your plebtap is ready for using.

Disconnet the USB and Test the plebtap on a USB powersource.
